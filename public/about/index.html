About East River Fragments

This project raises awareness about microplastic pollution in New York City's waterways, specifically focusing on the East River near Battery Park.

The Data

All statistics and information are sourced from:

  • NYC Department of Environmental Protection reports
  • Peer-reviewed scientific studies on harbor contamination
  • Environmental monitoring data from local organizations
