What You Can Do

Individual Actions

Reduce Single-Use Plastics

  • Carry a reusable water bottle and coffee cup
  • Use cloth bags for shopping
  • Choose products with minimal plastic packaging

Conscious Consumption

  • Buy natural fiber clothing when possible
  • Choose solid soap bars over liquid in plastic bottles
  • Support companies with sustainable packaging

Community Involvement

Local Cleanup

  • Join Battery Park cleanup events
  • Participate in river monitoring programs
  • Support local environmental organizations

Education & Advocacy

  • Share knowledge about microplastics
  • Contact representatives about plastic pollution policies
  • Support recycling infrastructure improvements

Every small action creates ripples of change.
